Remove CD-ROM

In the VirtualBox software, click on the "Settings" icon, then on the "Storage" icon, and after that on the small CD icon on the left sidebar, close to "Controller: IDE". Then click on the small CD icon on the right side, close to "Attributes", and select "Remove disk from virtual drive". This step is necessary so that the next time the virtual machine is booting, it will boot from the hard disk instead from the CD.
